Recruitments'24

Greetings from QCG! We are on the lookout for enthusiastic students interested in Quantum Computing and promoting its culture on campus. We will be recruiting for three verticals: Core Member (written test based on fundamentals of QC), Designer (for designing creative posts & our website), Web Developer (for maintaining & developing QCG Website). Scan the QR code or click on the link in bio to register latest by 20 Jan. For Core Member, written test is on 21st January, 2024 Time: 3:30 PM Venue: L2-005 For Designer and WebD verticals, assignments will be sent via email on 21st Jan along with submission details and deadline. Resources for all our discussions and basics of QC are available on our website (Link in Bio) Not sure what we do? Check out our intro talk slides (link in bio) for an overview of QCG and its activities.

Greetings from Greetings, everyone! After hours of intense brainstorming and dedicated learning, the Dead & Alive Hackathon has come to a close, leaving us with a collection of brilliant projects and innovative ideas.
